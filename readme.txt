== Description ==

Genwi Comments automatically adds comments about your blog posts from users on Genwi to your comments on your blog.


== Installation ==

Setup Instructions    1. Download and unzip the package    1. Copy the unzipped files to your wordpress plugin directory.    1. Open the wordpress admin dashboard, click on plugins, and activate the Genwi comments plugin.== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I have to have a Genwi account? =

Nope. But your blog does have to listed on Genwi.  If it is not listed with Genwi, please create a Genwi account and add your blog.
